What Is Future Funk?

Future funk is an upbeat genre reminiscent of French house music. It combines elements of funk, disco, and electronic music to create a sound that feels both nostalgic and modern. This style often involves sampling and reinterpreting classic tracks from the 70s and 80s, adding energetic rhythms, funky basslines, and edited vocals. Originating as a subgenre of vaporwave, which is slower and more distorted with heavy sampling, future funk has evolved into its own distinct sound. Overall, future funk is a genre that aims to capture the lost romance and emotions of dance music from the past.

What Is Future Funk Central?

Future Funk Central is a site solely dedicated to the genre of Future Funk. While the primary goal of this concept is to express my passion for the music genre in an entertaining manner, I also aim to encapsulate the essence of Future Funk. My vision for Future Funk Central is to create a platform where individuals can explore, educate themselves about, and relish in what Future Funk has to offer. Ultimately, I aspire for this website to serve as a hub for promoting the spirit of Future Funk and uniting like-minded individuals who appreciate the genre. Future Funk Central welcomes you to enjoy your stay at your one stop place for everything groovy.

Artist Spotlight:

Skylar Spence!

Skylar Spence, who was formerly known as Saint Pepsi, is an artist often accredited for solidifying and disseminating both the future funk and vaporwave genres. His music style is often regarded to invoke a feeling of nostalgia, calmness, and curiosity. He often combines samples from pop, funk, and disco songs with unorthodox sounds games or ambience.
